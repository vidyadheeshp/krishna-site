Visitor Information

Dress Code

Modest, traditional attire preferred. Remove footwear before entering the sanctum. Separate changing rooms available.

Photography

Photography permitted in outer areas. Please switch off flash and maintain silence inside the sanctum sanctorum.

Parking

Free parking available for 200 vehicles. Accessible parking near the main entrance. Electric vehicle charging point available.

Prasad & Annadanam

Free Prasad distributed after major aartis. Annadanam (free meals) available at noon daily for all pilgrims.

Accessibility

Wheelchair ramps, accessible restrooms, and assisted darshan available. Please call ahead for special arrangements.

Accommodation

Dharamshalas and budget guesthouses are available near the temple. Contact us for recommended accommodation partners.
