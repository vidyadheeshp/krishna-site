Janmashtami 2026 — Grand Celebration

The grand celebration of Lord Krishna's divine birth begins with special poojas from dawn, culminating in the spectacular midnight aarti marking the exact moment of His birth. Enjoy Ras Leela performances, Chhappan Bhog display, bhajan concerts, and free prasad distribution throughout the day.

August 16, 2026 5:00 AM – 12:00 midnight+ Main Temple Grounds Free Entry

Radhashtami — Appearance Day of Srimati Radharani

Celebrate the divine appearance day of Srimati Radharani — the most beloved devotee of Lord Krishna. Special abhishekam, beautiful flower decorations, devotional music, and extended evening aarti make this a deeply sacred occasion.

August 28, 2026 5:30 AM – 9:00 PM Radha Shrine Free Entry

Festival — 9 Days

Shardiya Navratri 2026

Nine nights of devotion dedicated to the divine feminine. Each evening features special Devi poojas, Garba performances, bhajan concerts, and spiritual discourses. The 9-day programme concludes with Dussehra celebrations.

Oct 2 – Oct 10, 2026 6:00 PM daily Temple Auditorium Free Entry

Festival

Govardhan Puja & Annakut Mahotsav

The magnificent Annakut display of 108 food offerings to Lord Krishna followed by free Prasad distribution to all devotees. Witness the spectacular mountain of food prepared by hundreds of devoted volunteers from the temple community.

October 21, 2026 8:00 AM – 4:00 PM Temple Grounds Free Prasad

Spiritual Discourse

Srimad Bhagavatam 7-Day Katha

A week-long recitation and discourse on Srimad Bhagavatam by renowned Vaishnava scholar Pandit Sri Gopalji Maharaj. Explore the divine pastimes of Lord Krishna and receive spiritual wisdom for daily life.

Nov 1 – Nov 7, 2026 10:00 AM – 1:00 PM Temple Hall Free Entry

Diwali Deepotsav 2026

Celebrate the festival of lights with a breathtaking display of 10,000 diyas (oil lamps) illuminating the entire temple complex. Special Lakshmi puja, fireworks, and a grand evening aarti mark this joyous occasion.

October 20, 2026 5:00 PM – 10:00 PM Full Temple Complex Free Entry
