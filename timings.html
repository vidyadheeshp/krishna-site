Please Note

Visitor Guidelines

Footwear

All footwear must be removed before entering the temple premises. Shoe stands are available near the main gate.

Dress Code

Modest, traditional dress is preferred. Dhoti/saree for special occasions. Avoid Western wear during special poojas.

Mobile Phones

Photography is permitted in the outer courtyard. Mobile phones must be on silent mode inside the main sanctum.

Prasad

Temple prasad (blessed food) is available after every aarti. Free Annadanam (community meal) is served daily at 12:30 PM.

Donations

Hundi (donation box) is available near the main shrine. All donations are used for temple maintenance and social services.

Enquiries

Temple office is open Mon–Sat: 9 AM – 5 PM. For special seva bookings, contact us at least 3 days in advance.

🛕

Ekadashi Special Timings

On every Ekadashi (11th lunar day, twice monthly), the temple extends its morning hours from 3:30 AM with a special pre-dawn Mangala Aarti, followed by extended Vishnu Sahasranama parayanam. The temple remains open throughout the day until 10 PM.
